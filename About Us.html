About Us Page

Chop Vault is a virtual food ordering website, which takes your order and deliver to you location. It is a major distributor of food items from Native Kitchen, Vegas Restaurant, Mercury Restaurant & Relaxation Center, Munchy Cruncy and Eco Chef Restaurant. It offers this service, having the interest of students and staff our great institution at heart.


Chop vault aims at eradicating the stress of moving to a distant location only to care for the stomach. It brings your favourite dish to your door step. It was established during the final week of the DSC Ae-Funai 6 week program. It is made up of a team of 7 developers Lead by James.
